## 🎯 About the project

Video-Studio-Eylon helps you create high-quality videos using artificial intelligence. You do not need video editing skills or complex software to produce professional clips. The application provides a simple web interface where you manage your assets, write scripts, and choose voiceovers. It supports right-to-left languages such as Hebrew, which makes it effective for localized video production.

The tool handles the technical rendering process through Remotion. This means you get clean, high-definition videos without managing frame rates or complex export settings. You upload your media, select your configuration, and the system generates your final video file.

## 🛠️ How to create your first video

When you open the application, a local web window appears. This window hosts your control panel. 

### Setting your project
Upon starting, the interface asks for a project name. Type a title for your video and choose a folder on your computer where the software saves your work. 

### Adding content
Use the "Upload" button to add images or video clips from your hard drive. The software organizes these files in the gallery view. You can drag and drop these items into your timeline to organize the order of your video.

### Writing your script
If you want to use the AI voiceover feature, type your text into the script box. The application supports Hebrew and other languages. Select your preferred voice style from the drop-down menu. The software processes this text and matches the audio timing to your video length.

### Adjusting the layout
The "Display" settings allow you to change how items appear on the screen. If you write in Hebrew, ensure you select the "RTL" or Right-to-Left mode to align your text and layout correctly. These settings apply to all slides or clips in your current project.

### Rendering the final file
Once you arrange your clips and text, click the "Render" button. The application uses your computer's resources to build the video file. You can see a progress bar during this time. Do not close the window while the bar moves. When the process finishes, the software notifies you and opens the folder containing your final video.

## 💡 Troubleshooting common issues

If you encounter problems, check these common fixes:

*   Application does not open: Ensure no other instances of the program are running. Check your task manager to close any hung processes.
*   Slow rendering: Close other web browsers or memory-heavy programs while you export your video. 
*   Missing audio: Verify that your computer volume is up and check the voiceover selection in the script menu.
*   Video file not found: Check the project folder you selected during the initial setup. The software saves all rendered outputs in a subfolder named "Exports."
